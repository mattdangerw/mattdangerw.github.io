Matthew Watson

Machine Learning Software Engineer

Hello! I am a software engineer at Google working on Reinforcement Learning for the Gemini model. I'm also a core maintainer of the Keras deep learning library and co-author of the Deep Learning with Python book.

Almost an English major, I'm interested in fuzzy human problems and their intersection with technology. I am deeply interested in improving our fundamental understanding of language models, and making the field more accessible as a whole.
